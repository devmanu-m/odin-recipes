Tropical Mango-Banana Recipe

Description

The Tropical Mango Banana Smoothie is a creamy, refreshing blend of ripe mangoes and bananas, perfect for hot days or a nutritious breakfast. Packed with natural sweetness and tropical flavor, it combines fruits with yogurt or milk for a smooth, energizing drink. With optional touches of honey, ginger, or cinnamon, it's both delicious and customizable to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1/2 cup cold water or ice cubes
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • A dash of cinnamon or fresh ginger (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine mango, banana, yogurt (or milk), and water (or ice) in a blender.
  2. Add honey and cinnamon or ginger if using.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
