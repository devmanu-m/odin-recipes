Skumawiki Recipe

Description

Skumawiki, well known as skuma by it's locals, is a common dish prepared for lunch and even dinner.

Below are the ingredients required for its preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (collard greens), finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 stock cube (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • A pinch of black pepper or royco (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a sufuria over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions (and garlic if using) and saut√© until soft.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down into a thick sauce.
  4. Add the chopped sukuma wiki. Stir everything together to coat the greens in the tomato-onion mix.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and stock cube if using.
  6. Cover and cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the greens are tender but still bright green.
  7. Serve hot with ugali or as a side to your stew.
